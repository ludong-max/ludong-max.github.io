One-sentence problem: How can we capture long-form, unscripted egocentric cooking activity in real home kitchens at scale, with dense action, audio, segmentation, and 3D annotations to benchmark diverse video understanding tasks?
一句话问题：如何在真实家庭厨房中大规模采集长时无脚本的自我中心烹饪活动，并配以密集的动作、音频、分割和 3D 标注，用于多样化视频理解基准测试？

Benchmark Tasks & Annual Challenges

Action Recognition Action Detection Action Anticipation Multi-Instance Retrieval Unsupervised Domain Adaptation Video Object Segmentation (VISOR) Audio Recognition (EPIC-Sounds) Audio Detection (EPIC-Sounds) HD-EPIC VQA
  • Action recognition: Predict verb + noun class from untrimmed egocentric video clips; evaluated on seen/unseen kitchen splits to test temporal generalization.
    动作识别：从未修剪的自我中心视频片段预测动词+名词类别；在已见/未见厨房划分上测试时序泛化能力。
  • Action anticipation: Predict the next action from past observations — a unique challenge in EPIC-KITCHENS that requires modeling long-range cooking intent.
    动作预测：从过去观测预测下一个动作——EPIC-KITCHENS 的独特挑战，需要对长程烹饪意图建模。
  • Multi-instance retrieval: Retrieve all video clips matching a text query (or vice versa), evaluated with mAP over ranked lists — requires fine-grained action-language alignment.
    多实例检索：检索与文本查询匹配的所有视频片段（反之亦然），用排序列表的 mAP 评估——需要细粒度动作-语言对齐。
  • HD-EPIC VQA (2025 new): Answer questions across 7 categories: recipes, ingredients, nutrition, actions, 3D perception, object motion, gaze — leveraging digital twin kitchen reconstructions.
    HD-EPIC VQA（2025 新增）：回答 7 类问题：食谱、食材、营养、动作、3D 感知、物体运动、注视——借助数字孪生厨房重建。

✅ 直接投主会 / 期刊

  • EPIC-KITCHENS 是 CVPR / ICCV / ECCV / NeurIPS 动作识别、视频理解、audio-visual 类论文的常用基准。
  • 新任务方向：结合 HD-EPIC 3D 数字孪生做 egocentric 3D scene understanding、或用 EPIC-Sounds 做 audio-visual 动作识别，目前竞争相对 Ego4D 小。

⚠️ 注意

  • EPIC-KITCHENS 没有 SMPL-X 或全身姿态标注，不适合直接用于 body regression 任务。
  • VISOR 提供手-物体分割，可用于手部分析相关论文（hand regression 下游评估）。
  • HD-EPIC 是 2025 新数据集，挑战赛刚起步，早期参与论文容易形成 baseline 影响力。

TL;DR

EPIC-KITCHENS is a family of egocentric kitchen datasets anchored by the 100-hour EPIC-KITCHENS-100 (IJCV 2022), extended by VISOR (segmentation), EPIC-Sounds (audio), EPIC-Fields (3D NeRF), and the new HD-EPIC (CVPR 2025, 41h with digital twin scenes + VQA). An annual challenge runs at EgoVis@CVPR — good opportunity for workshop papers on recognition, anticipation, retrieval, and VQA. No SMPL-X annotations — limited direct use for body regression, but VISOR hand masks can support hand analysis.
