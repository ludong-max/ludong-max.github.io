Paper Digest · arXiv 2311.18259 · CVPR 2024

Ego-Exo4D: Understanding Skilled Human Activity from First- and Third-Person Perspectives

Kristen Grauman, Andrew Westbury, Lorenzo Torresani, Kris Kitani, Jitendra Malik, + 96 collaborators
Meta AI · CMU · Dartmouth · UC Berkeley · and many more
Public Dataset Egocentric Ego + Exo Skilled Activities Multimodal
One-sentence problem: How can we build a large-scale benchmark that enables AI systems to understand skilled human activities by jointly leveraging both egocentric (first-person) and exocentric (third-person) video perspectives?
一句话问题：如何构建一个大规模基准，使 AI 系统能够同时利用第一人称（自我中心）和第三人称（外部视角）视频来理解技巧性人类活动？

Contributions

  • Largest ego+exo synchronized dataset: 1,286 hours of video from 740 participants across 13 cities worldwide, with simultaneous egocentric and multiple exocentric camera recordings of skilled activities (sports, music, cooking, dance, etc.).
    最大规模 ego+exo 同步数据集：740 名参与者在全球 13 座城市完成技巧性活动（体育、音乐、烹饪、舞蹈等），共 1,286 小时同步自我中心与多视角外部视频。
  • Rich multimodal annotations: Includes multichannel audio, IMU data, eye gaze, 3D point clouds, narrations, and novel expert-novice commentary describing skill proficiency and coaching cues.
    丰富多模态标注：包含多声道音频、IMU、眼动追踪、3D 点云、文字叙述，以及新颖的专家-新手评论，描述技能水平和指导提示。
  • Diverse benchmark suite: Seven benchmark tasks spanning keystep recognition, proficiency estimation, relation detection, ego-exo cross-view translation, pose estimation, and 3D reconstruction.
    多样化基准任务：七项基准任务，涵盖关键步骤识别、技能水平估计、关系检测、自我-外部跨视角转换、姿态估计和 3D 重建。
  • Open infrastructure: Fully released dataset, annotation tools, and baseline models to enable community-wide research in first-person video understanding.
    开源基础设施：完整发布数据集、标注工具和基线模型，推动第一人称视频理解的社区研究。

Benchmark Tasks

  • Keystep recognition: Recognize fine-grained procedural steps from egocentric video (e.g., "add salt", "strum chord").
    关键步骤识别：从自我中心视频识别细粒度操作步骤（如"加盐"、"拨弦"）。
  • Proficiency estimation: Predict skill level (novice→expert) from video, using expert coaching annotations as supervision signal.
    技能水平估计：利用专家评论标注预测视频中表演者的技能水平（新手→专家）。
  • Ego-to-Exo / Exo-to-Ego translation: Synthesize or retrieve the complementary perspective given one viewpoint.
    自我-外部视角转换：给定一种视角，合成或检索互补视角。
  • 3D pose estimation: Estimate full-body pose from synchronized ego+exo streams using shared 3D point cloud scaffolding.
    3D 姿态估计：利用同步 ego+exo 流和共享 3D 点云骨架估计全身姿态。

⚠️ Note

Ego-Exo4D provides 3D joints, not SMPL-X. So it can support 3D pose supervision/evaluation, but an extra SMPL-X fitting step is needed.

TL;DR

Ego-Exo4D is a 1,286-hour multimodal dataset capturing skilled human activities from synchronized egocentric and exocentric cameras across 13 cities and 740 participants. Its unique expert-novice commentary annotations and seven diverse benchmarks make it the de-facto standard for studying first-person activity understanding and ego-exo video correspondence.
