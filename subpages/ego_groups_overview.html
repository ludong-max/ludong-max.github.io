Egocentric Data — Who Else Is Building It?

Beyond Meta / Project Aria, several major groups — industry labs, academic consortia, and new tech entrants — are actively building large-scale egocentric datasets. This page gives a quick comparative overview of each group's focus, signature datasets, and trajectory.
除 Meta / Project Aria 之外，多个主要团队——工业实验室、学术联合体和新兴科技公司——正在积极构建大规模自我中心数据集。本页提供各团队重点方向、代表性数据集及研究走向的快速对比概览。

Landscape Analysis

Industry giants — two camps:

  • Meta is the clear leader in hardware-anchored egocentric data — Aria glasses give them proprietary sensor quality (SLAM, eye tracking, IMU) no one else matches at scale. Their Nymeria/NymeriaPlus datasets are the go-to for full-body SMPL regression training data.
  • Microsoft leverages HoloLens for mixed-reality task guidance data (HoloAssist), but at a smaller scale. Their newer direction — using egocentric human video for robotic VLA pretraining — is more impactful in the embodied AI direction.
  • Apple (Vision Pro) has not released any public egocentric dataset. Their research group works on AR/3D internally, but nothing comparable to Aria is open.
  • Google / DeepMind focus more on robotics (RT-2, OpenX) and video understanding (Gemini), but no dedicated egocentric dataset program.
  • NVIDIA is involved via RoboSpatial (3D spatial understanding for robotics using egocentric images) but not running a primary egocentric data program.

Academic leaders:

  • Bristol (Damen) built the most sustainable community around EPIC-Kitchens with an annual challenge — best venue for workshop papers and benchmark comparisons.
  • ETH Zurich (Tang/Pollefeys) is the top academic group for 3D body understanding from egocentric views, with SMPL-X as the output format — most directly useful for body regression research.
  • CMU (Kitani) / Berkeley (Malik) are key academic PIs in the Ego4D/Ego-Exo4D consortium.

New entrant to watch: Alibaba/InternRobotics (EgoRe-5M, 13M clips) signals that Chinese AI labs are entering the egocentric space at scale, specifically targeting video-language reasoning rather than MoCap-style annotation.

工业巨头——两大阵营：

  • Meta 是硬件驱动自我中心数据领域的绝对领先者——Aria 眼镜提供任何人都无法在规模上匹敌的专有传感器质量（SLAM、眼动追踪、IMU）。Nymeria/NymeriaPlus 是全身 SMPL 回归训练数据的首选。
  • Microsoft 利用 HoloLens 构建混合现实任务指导数据（HoloAssist），但规模较小。其更新的方向——用自我中心人类视频进行机器人 VLA 预训练——在具身 AI 方向影响力更大。
  • Apple（Vision Pro）尚未发布任何公开自我中心数据集。其研究团队内部开展 AR/3D 研究，但没有任何可与 Aria 媲美的开放项目。
  • Google / DeepMind 更专注于机器人学（RT-2、OpenX）和视频理解（Gemini），没有专用的自我中心数据集项目。
  • NVIDIA 通过 RoboSpatial（使用自我中心图像的机器人 3D 空间理解）参与其中，但没有主要的自我中心数据项目。

学术领军团队：

  • Bristol（Damen）通过年度挑战赛围绕 EPIC-Kitchens 构建了最具持续性的社区——是发表 workshop 论文和基准对比的最佳平台。
  • ETH Zurich（Tang/Pollefeys）是从自我中心视角进行 3D 人体理解的顶级学术团队，以 SMPL-X 作为输出格式——对体态回归研究最直接有用。
  • CMU（Kitani）/ Berkeley（Malik）是 Ego4D/Ego-Exo4D 联合体的核心学术 PI。

值得关注的新兴力量：阿里巴巴/书生机器人（EgoRe-5M，1300 万片段）表明中国 AI 实验室正在大规模进入自我中心领域，专门针对视频-语言推理而非传统动捕式标注。

TL;DR

Meta dominates egocentric data at scale and quality. Bristol owns the kitchen benchmark community. ETH Zurich leads academic 3D body understanding with SMPL-X. Microsoft covers HoloLens-based task guidance. Apple and Google have no public egocentric datasets. Alibaba is the new large-scale entrant focusing on video-language reasoning. For body regression research, Meta (Nymeria) and ETH (EgoBody) are the most relevant sources of SMPL-X compatible egocentric training data.
Meta 在规模和质量上主导自我中心数据领域。Bristol 拥有厨房基准社区。ETH Zurich 以 SMPL-X 引领学术 3D 人体理解。Microsoft 覆盖基于 HoloLens 的任务指导。Apple 和 Google 没有公开的自我中心数据集。阿里巴巴是专注视频-语言推理的新兴大规模参与者。对于体态回归研究，Meta（Nymeria）和 ETH（EgoBody）是最相关的 SMPL-X 兼容自我中心训练数据来源。
