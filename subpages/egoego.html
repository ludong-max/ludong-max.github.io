One-sentence problem: Can we estimate full-body 3D human motion from monocular egocentric video вҖ” where the body is almost never visible вҖ” by using head motion as an intermediate representation, without requiring paired egocentric video + 3D motion training data?

Key Contributions

  • EgoEgo two-stage decomposition: Stage 1 estimates head pose from egocentric video (SLAM + learned GravityNet + HeadNet); Stage 2 generates full-body SMPL motion conditioned on the head pose via conditional diffusion. Each stage can be trained on separate single-modality datasets.
    EgoEgo дёӨйҳ¶ж®өеҲҶи§Јпјҡз¬¬дёҖйҳ¶ж®өд»ҺиҮӘжҲ‘дёӯеҝғи§Ҷйў‘дј°и®ЎеӨҙйғЁе§ҝжҖҒпјҲSLAM + еӯҰд№ еһӢ GravityNet + HeadNetпјүпјӣз¬¬дәҢйҳ¶ж®өйҖҡиҝҮжқЎд»¶жү©ж•Јд»ҘеӨҙйғЁе§ҝжҖҒдёәжқЎд»¶з”ҹжҲҗе…Ёиә« SMPL иҝҗеҠЁгҖӮжҜҸдёӘйҳ¶ж®өеҸҜеңЁзӢ¬з«Ӣзҡ„еҚ•жЁЎжҖҒж•°жҚ®йӣҶдёҠи®ӯз»ғгҖӮ
  • Hybrid head pose estimation: Addresses three SLAM failure modes (unknown gravity, non-metric scale, rotation errors) with GravityNet (gravity direction from AMASS) and HeadNet (scale + rotation from optical flow via ResNet-18 + Transformer). GravityNet trained on AMASS; HeadNet on ARES/Kinpoly-RealWorld/GIMO.

Method Architecture

Stage 1 вҖ” Head Pose from Egocentric Video Egocentric video I вҶ’ DROID-SLAM (raw rotation + translation) вҶ’ GravityNet (Transformer, predicts g вҲҲ RВі вҶ’ rotation correction R_g) + HeadNet (optical flow o_t вҶ’ distance d_t + rotation R_t) вҶ’ Head pose H вҲҲ R^{TГ—D'}
Stage 2 вҖ” Full-Body from Head Pose (Conditional Diffusion) Head pose H + noise x_N вҶ’ Transformer denoising network (DDPM, conditioned on H) вҶ’ Full-body SMPL X вҲҲ R^{TГ—D} (22 joints, pos + rot)
Training decoupling insight: GravityNet + HeadNet train on egocentric videos without needing 3D body labels. The diffusion body generator trains on AMASS motion capture without needing egocentric video. No paired data required вҖ” only the two stages need to share the head pose interface format.

TL;DR

EgoEgo (CVPR 2023, Stanford) is the direct precursor to EgoAllo. It establishes the core paradigm: head pose as intermediate representation вҶ’ conditional diffusion for full-body SMPL. The key innovation is decoupled training вҖ” Stage 1 (GravityNet + HeadNet on top of DROID-SLAM) uses video only; Stage 2 (DDPM Transformer) uses AMASS MoCap only. Achieves MPJPE 121mm on ARES vs 142mm (Kinpoly-OF). Main gap vs EgoAllo: camera-space only (no world-space), no hand estimation, and head pose errors are the main bottleneck (GT head improves body by 12%).
