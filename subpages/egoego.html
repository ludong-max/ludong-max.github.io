EgoEgo: Ego-Body Pose Estimation via Ego-Head Pose Estimation

Jiaman Li, C. Karen Liu†, Jiajun Wu†
Stanford University
SMPL Body Regression Egocentric Conditional Diffusion SLAM Unpaired Training CVPR 2023
📄 arXiv PDF 📋 CVPR PDF Project GitHub
One-sentence problem: Can we estimate full-body 3D human motion from monocular egocentric video — where the body is almost never visible — by using head motion as an intermediate representation, without requiring paired egocentric video + 3D motion training data?
一句话问题：能否通过将头部运动作为中间表示，从单目自我中心视频（身体几乎不可见）估计全身 3D 人体运动——且无需配对的自我中心视频 + 3D 动作训练数据？
⚠️ 关系定位： EgoEgo 是 EgoAllo (ECCV 2024) 的直接前身——同样是"头部 → 全身扩散"两阶段范式，但仅输出相机坐标系的 SMPL 体态，不含手部，不含世界坐标系轨迹。

Key Contributions

  • EgoEgo two-stage decomposition: Stage 1 estimates head pose from egocentric video (SLAM + learned GravityNet + HeadNet); Stage 2 generates full-body SMPL motion conditioned on the head pose via conditional diffusion. Each stage can be trained on separate single-modality datasets.
    EgoEgo 两阶段分解：第一阶段从自我中心视频估计头部姿态（SLAM + 学习型 GravityNet + HeadNet）；第二阶段通过条件扩散以头部姿态为条件生成全身 SMPL 运动。每个阶段可在独立的单模态数据集上训练。
  • Hybrid head pose estimation: Addresses three SLAM failure modes (unknown gravity, non-metric scale, rotation errors) with GravityNet (gravity direction from AMASS) and HeadNet (scale + rotation from optical flow via ResNet-18 + Transformer). GravityNet trained on AMASS; HeadNet on ARES/Kinpoly-RealWorld/GIMO.
    混合头部姿态估计：用 GravityNet（从 AMASS 估计重力方向）和 HeadNet（从光流估计尺度 + 旋转，ResNet-18 + Transformer）解决 SLAM 三大失效模式（未知重力、非公制尺度、旋转误差）。
  • Conditional diffusion body generator: DDPM-based Transformer denoising network conditioned on head pose sequence H ∈ R^{T×D'}; generates diverse plausible full-body SMPL motions (22 joints, global position + rotation). Trained on AMASS alone — no egocentric video needed.
    条件扩散体态生成器：基于 DDPM 的 Transformer 去噪网络，以头部姿态序列 H ∈ R^{T×D'} 为条件；生成多样合理的全身 SMPL 运动（22 关节，全局位置 + 旋转）。仅在 AMASS 上训练——无需自我中心视频。
  • ARES synthetic benchmark: AMASS-Replica-Ego-Syn — 1.66M frames (~15h) across 18 indoor scenes, paired egocentric video + 3D motion, generated via AI Habitat renderer. Enables systematic benchmarking without real paired capture.
    ARES 合成基准：AMASS-Replica-Ego-Syn——18 个室内场景 166 万帧（约 15 小时），配对自我中心视频 + 3D 运动，通过 AI Habitat 渲染器生成。无需真实配对采集即可系统性评测。

Method Architecture

Stage 1 — Head Pose from Egocentric Video Egocentric video I → DROID-SLAM (raw rotation + translation) → GravityNet (Transformer, predicts g ∈ R³ → rotation correction R_g) + HeadNet (optical flow o_t → distance d_t + rotation R_t) → Head pose H ∈ R^{T×D'}
Stage 2 — Full-Body from Head Pose (Conditional Diffusion) Head pose H + noise x_N → Transformer denoising network (DDPM, conditioned on H) → Full-body SMPL X ∈ R^{T×D} (22 joints, pos + rot)
Training decoupling insight: GravityNet + HeadNet train on egocentric videos without needing 3D body labels. The diffusion body generator trains on AMASS motion capture without needing egocentric video. No paired data required — only the two stages need to share the head pose interface format.
训练解耦思路：GravityNet + HeadNet 在自我中心视频上训练，无需 3D 体态标签。扩散体态生成器在 AMASS 动捕数据上训练，无需自我中心视频。不需要配对数据——两个阶段只需共享头部姿态接口格式。

Key takeaway: head pose accuracy directly bottlenecks body quality — improving head estimation from predicted (T_head=148mm) to GT (T_head=29mm) reduces MPJPE from 121mm to 106mm, a 12% gain. This motivates EgoAllo's improved SLAM conditioning.
关键结论：头部姿态精度直接制约体态质量——将头部从预测（T_head=148mm）改善到真值（T_head=29mm）时，MPJPE 从 121mm 降至 106mm（提升 12%）。这为 EgoAllo 改进 SLAM 条件化提供了动机。

Limitations

  • Camera-space body only: EgoEgo estimates SMPL-style 22-joint body motion in the camera/head coordinate setting. It does not recover world-space / allocentric trajectories that can be directly anchored in an AR scene.
    仅相机坐标系身体：EgoEgo 估计的是相机/头部坐标设定下的 22 关节 SMPL 式身体运动，不恢复可直接锚定到 AR 场景的世界坐标系 / 异中心轨迹。
  • No articulated hands or face: The method does not estimate MANO hands, FLAME face, or unified SMPL-X parameters. It is useful as a body-motion precursor, not a complete whole-body avatar solution.
    没有精细手部或面部：该方法不估计 MANO 手部、FLAME 面部或统一 SMPL-X 参数。它适合作为身体运动前序方法，而不是完整 whole-body avatar 方案。
  • Head pose is the bottleneck: The diffusion body generator depends heavily on Stage-1 head pose. The paper's own ablation shows that using GT head pose reduces ARES MPJPE from 121.1mm to 105.6mm, so SLAM/HeadNet errors directly cap final body quality.
    头部姿态是瓶颈：扩散体态生成器强依赖第一阶段头部姿态。论文消融显示，使用真值头部姿态可将 ARES MPJPE 从 121.1mm 降到 105.6mm，因此 SLAM/HeadNet 误差会直接限制最终体态质量。
  • Synthetic benchmark dependency: ARES is valuable for systematic evaluation, but it is rendered from AMASS + Replica rather than captured in the wild. Real-world generalization is still tested on limited datasets such as Kinpoly and GIMO.
    依赖合成基准：ARES 对系统评测很有价值，但它由 AMASS + Replica 渲染生成，不是真实野外采集。真实泛化仍主要依赖 Kinpoly 和 GIMO 等规模有限的数据集验证。
  • Generative but not real-time focused: Stage 2 is a conditional diffusion model and quantitative evaluation samples multiple motions from the same head pose. This improves plausibility but is not optimized for low-latency headset deployment.
    生成式但不以实时为主：第二阶段是条件扩散模型，定量评测会从同一头部姿态采样多个动作。这提升合理性，但并非针对低延迟头显部署优化。

TL;DR

EgoEgo (CVPR 2023, Stanford) is the direct precursor to EgoAllo. It establishes the core paradigm: head pose as intermediate representation → conditional diffusion for full-body SMPL. The key innovation is decoupled training — Stage 1 (GravityNet + HeadNet on top of DROID-SLAM) uses video only; Stage 2 (DDPM Transformer) uses AMASS MoCap only. Achieves MPJPE 121mm on ARES vs 142mm (Kinpoly-OF). Main gap vs EgoAllo: camera-space only (no world-space), no hand estimation, and head pose errors are the main bottleneck (GT head improves body by 12%).
