Estimating Body and Hand Motion in an Ego-sensed World

Brent Yi, Vickie Ye, Maya Zheng, Yunqi Li, Lea Müller, Georgios Pavlakos, Yi Ma, Jitendra Malik, Angjoo Kanazawa
UC Berkeley · ETH Zurich
SMPL-X Body Regression Hand Estimation Egocentric Diffusion Model SLAM
One-sentence problem: Given only egocentric video from a head-mounted device (with SLAM poses), can we recover the wearer's full-body pose and hand parameters in the world/allocentric coordinate frame?
一句话问题：仅给定头戴设备的自我中心视频（含 SLAM 位姿），能否在世界/异中心坐标系下恢复佩戴者的全身姿态和手部参数？

Contributions

  • EgoAllo system: A conditional diffusion model that estimates allocentric full-body SMPL pose (body height + joint angles) and MANO hand parameters from egocentric SLAM trajectories and RGB images.
    EgoAllo 系统：以自我中心 SLAM 轨迹和 RGB 图像为条件，用条件扩散模型估计异中心全身 SMPL 姿态（身高 + 关节角度）和 MANO 手部参数。
  • Head-motion conditioning (+18%): Derives a novel parameterization for head motion that enforces spatial and temporal invariance, improving body estimation accuracy by up to 18% over naive conditioning.
    头部运动条件化（+18%）：提出一种新的头部运动参数化方式，满足空间和时间不变性准则，相比朴素条件化提升体态估计精度最高 18%。
  • Kinematic constraints for hands (−40% error): Uses the estimated body pose as a kinematic prior and applies temporal constraints, reducing hand pose error by 40% compared to hand-only baselines.
    手部运动学约束（误差 −40%）：将估计的体态作为运动学先验，结合时序约束，相比仅手部基线将手部姿态误差降低 40%。
  • Allocentric estimation: Unlike most methods that estimate body pose in camera space, EgoAllo recovers motion in the world coordinate frame, enabling direct use in AR/VR and scene interaction applications.
    异中心估计：与大多数在相机坐标系下估计的方法不同，EgoAllo 在世界坐标系中恢复运动，可直接用于 AR/VR 和场景交互应用。

Method Overview

Input: egocentric RGB video + SLAM 6-DoF head trajectory → Output: allocentric SMPL body + MANO hands (per-frame, in world frame)

Step 1 — Head conditioning: The SLAM trajectory is re-parameterized into a spatially and temporally invariant representation (relative velocity + orientation residuals). This removes absolute-position and time-shift ambiguity that degrades naive conditioning.

Step 2 — Body diffusion: A denoising diffusion transformer takes the head conditioning + RGB features and predicts a distribution over SMPL body parameters in world space. Multiple samples can be drawn for uncertainty estimation.

Step 3 — Hand estimation: The predicted wrist poses from Step 2 serve as anchors. A separate network estimates MANO parameters conditioned on both the wrist pose priors and temporal context, enforcing kinematic consistency.

Step 4 — Temporal smoothing: A learned temporal filter refines per-frame estimates to reduce jitter while preserving fast motions.

输入：自我中心 RGB 视频 + SLAM 6-DoF 头部轨迹 → 输出：世界坐标系下的逐帧 SMPL 全身 + MANO 手部参数

步骤 1 — 头部条件化：将 SLAM 轨迹重新参数化为空间和时间不变表示（相对速度 + 方向残差），消除绝对位置和时间偏移的歧义。

步骤 2 — 体态扩散：去噪扩散 Transformer 以头部条件 + RGB 特征为输入，预测世界坐标系下 SMPL 体态参数的分布，可多次采样用于不确定性估计。

步骤 3 — 手部估计：步骤 2 预测的腕部位姿作为锚点，一个独立网络以腕部先验和时序上下文为条件估计 MANO 参数，强制运动学一致性。

步骤 4 — 时序平滑：学习的时序滤波器精化逐帧估计，减少抖动同时保留快速运动。

TL;DR

EgoAllo recovers allocentric full-body + hand motion from egocentric SLAM + RGB using conditional diffusion. Its key insight is a spatially and temporally invariant head-motion conditioning that improves body accuracy by 18%, combined with kinematic wrist anchoring that cuts hand error by 40% — enabling downstream AR/VR and scene-interaction use cases.
