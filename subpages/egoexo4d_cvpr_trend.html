CVPR Trend: Works Using Ego-Exo4D

Ego-Exo4D (CVPR 2024) · 36+ downstream papers across CVPR / ICCV / NeurIPS / ICLR
Cross-View Correspondence Proficiency Estimation Keystep Recognition Ego Pose View Translation
Since its CVPR 2024 release, Ego-Exo4D has spawned 36+ downstream papers at top venues. The dominant research thread is cross-view object correspondence / segmentation (ego↔exo). A second strong thread is expert coaching and proficiency estimation leveraging the dataset's unique expert commentary annotations.
自 CVPR 2024 发布以来，Ego-Exo4D 已衍生出在顶级会议发表的 36+ 篇下游论文。最主要的研究方向是跨视角物体对应/分割（ego↔exo）。第二条强线是利用数据集独特专家评论标注的专家指导与技能水平估计。

TL;DR — Research Trends

① Cross-view correspondence dominates — the ego↔exo object segmentation task has generated the most papers (5+), with ICCV 2025 highlights and a dedicated CVPRW 2025 challenge track.

② Expert commentary = unique value — Ego-Exo4D's novel expert coaching labels enable a new class of "AI coach" papers (ExpertAF @ CVPR 2025, ExpertEdit 2026) that no other dataset supports.

③ Pose track is growing — the ego pose benchmark attracts NeurIPS-level work on body/hand estimation from sparse egocentric video.

④ EgoVis Workshop @ CVPR is the main venue for challenge results — annual tech-report papers (2024 + 2025) make it low-barrier to publish with Ego-Exo4D results.
① 跨视角对应主导——ego↔exo 物体分割任务产生了最多论文（5+），ICCV 2025 有高亮论文，CVPRW 2025 有专属挑战赛赛道。

② 专家评论 = 独特价值——Ego-Exo4D 新颖的专家指导标签催生了一类"AI 教练"论文（ExpertAF @ CVPR 2025，ExpertEdit 2026），这是其他数据集无法支撑的。

③ 姿态赛道持续增长——自我中心姿态基准吸引了 NeurIPS 级别的从稀疏自我中心视频进行体态/手部估计的工作。

④ EgoVis Workshop @ CVPR 是挑战赛结果的主要发表平台——年度技术报告论文（2024 + 2025）发表门槛低，是利用 Ego-Exo4D 结果发文的好渠道。
