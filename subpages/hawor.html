HaWoR: World-Space Hand Motion Reconstruction from Egocentric Videos

Jinglei Zhang, Jiankang Deng, Chao Ma, Rolandos Alexandros Potamias
Imperial College London  ·  Shanghai Jiao Tong University （WiLoR 同一团队）
Hand Regression World-Space SLAM Temporal Egocentric arXiv 2025
📄 arXiv
One-sentence problem: Prior hand regression methods (HaMeR, WiLoR) recover MANO parameters in camera space — how do we reconstruct continuous, globally-consistent hand trajectories in world coordinates from egocentric video, even under severe occlusions?
一句话问题：HaMeR、WiLoR 等方法在相机坐标系下恢复 MANO 参数——如何从自我中心视频中重建世界坐标系下连续、全局一致的手部轨迹，即便在严重遮挡下也能有效处理？

Key Contributions

  • World-space decomposition: Separates the problem into (1) camera-frame MANO estimation and (2) camera trajectory recovery via egocentric SLAM — combining the two to produce world-space hand motion without joint optimization.
    世界坐标系分解：将问题拆分为（1）相机帧内 MANO 估计和（2）通过自我中心 SLAM 恢复相机轨迹——结合两者得到世界坐标系下的手部运动，无需联合优化。
  • Temporal attention modules (IAM + PAM): Image Attention Module aggregates features across adjacent frames to handle truncated/occluded hands; Pose Attention Module learns hand motion priors directly on MANO pose parameters — together cutting W-MPJPE from 44.6mm to 33.2mm vs no attention.
    时序注意力模块（IAM + PAM）：图像注意力模块跨相邻帧聚合特征以处理截断/遮挡手部；姿态注意力模块直接在 MANO 姿态参数上学习手部运动先验——二者合计将 W-MPJPE 从 44.6mm 降至 33.2mm。
  • Adaptive Egocentric SLAM: Dual-masking strategy excludes hand regions from DROID-SLAM's bundle adjustment (masking both input images and confidence maps) to prevent dynamic hand motion from corrupting camera trajectory. AdaSM module integrates Metric3D for scale estimation using only reliable non-hand scene points.
    自适应自我中心 SLAM：双掩码策略将手部区域排除在 DROID-SLAM 的 bundle adjustment 之外（同时掩码输入图像和置信度图），防止手部动态运动污染相机轨迹。AdaSM 模块利用 Metric3D，仅使用可靠的非手部场景点进行尺度估计。
  • Motion Infiller Network: Transformer-encoder in canonical space fills in world-space hand motion for occluded frames — reduces FID by 62.5% and W-MPJPE by 11.8% vs linear interpolation on invisible sequences.
    动作填充网络：正则坐标空间下的 Transformer 编码器为遮挡帧填充世界坐标系手部运动——相比线性插值，FID 降低 62.5%，W-MPJPE 降低 11.8%。

Method Architecture

Stage 1 — Camera-Space Hand Motion Egocentric video frames → WiLoR ViT backbone + IAM (cross-frame image attn) + PAM (pose sequence attn) → MANO (Θ, β, Φ, Γ) per frame
Stage 2 — Camera Trajectory (Adaptive SLAM) Frames → Dual-masked DROID-SLAM (hand regions excluded) + Metric3D + AdaSM (metric scale) → World camera poses T_t
Stage 3 — World-Space Fusion + Occlusion Filling MANO (camera) + T_t → World-space projection → Motion Infiller (canonical Transformer, fills occluded frames) → World-space hand trajectory
Training data: 1M frames from HOT3D (573K) + ARCTIC (165K) + DexYCB (169K) + HO3D (66K). Hand estimation trained 250K iterations (4× A800, AdamW lr=1e-5); Infiller trained 1.5M iterations. Runtime: 40ms/frame (vs 160ms for HMP-SLAM — 4× faster).
训练数据：来自 HOT3D（573K）+ ARCTIC（165K）+ DexYCB（169K）+ HO3D（66K）的 100 万帧。手部估计训练 25 万次迭代（4× A800，AdamW lr=1e-5）；填充网络训练 150 万次迭代。运行时间：40ms/帧（vs HMP-SLAM 的 160ms——快 4 倍）。

Limitations

  • Tracking identity failures: Relies on off-the-shelf hand detector; left/right hand confusion propagates errors throughout the trajectory.
    追踪身份失败：依赖现成手部检测器，左右手混淆会在整条轨迹中传播错误。
  • No inter-hand constraints: Each hand modeled independently — self-penetrations occur during hand-to-hand interaction sequences.
    无手间约束：每只手独立建模——双手交互序列中会出现自穿透现象。
  • Not real-time: 40ms/frame is still too slow for live AR/VR deployment despite being 4× faster than HMP-SLAM.
    非实时：尽管比 HMP-SLAM 快 4 倍，40ms/帧对实时 AR/VR 部署仍然太慢。

TL;DR

HaWoR (same group as WiLoR, arXiv Jan 2025) lifts hand regression from camera space to world space for egocentric video. It decomposes the problem: WiLoR-backbone + IAM/PAM temporal attention for camera-frame MANO, then adaptive dual-masked SLAM + Metric3D for camera trajectory, combined via a canonical-space Motion Infiller for occluded frames. Achieves W-MPJPE 33.2mm vs 156mm for HaMeR-SLAM (78.7% ↓) and RTE 0.78% vs 4.77% (84% ↓) on HOT3D, at 40ms/frame.
