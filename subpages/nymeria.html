Nymeria: A Massive Collection of Multimodal Egocentric Daily Motion in the Wild

Lingni Ma, Yuting Ye, Fangzhou Hong, Vladimir Guzov, Yifeng Jiang, Rowan Postyeni, …, Richard Newcombe
Meta Reality Labs · Stanford · NTU
Public Dataset Egocentric Full-Body Motion Multimodal
One-sentence problem: How do we collect a large-scale, in-the-wild human motion dataset that simultaneously captures egocentric sensor streams, accurate full-body ground truth, and rich language descriptions?
一句话问题：如何在真实户外场景中，同时采集大规模自我中心传感器数据、精确全身动作真值与丰富语言描述的人体运动数据集？

Contributions

  • Largest wild-collected motion dataset: 300 hours of daily activities from 264 participants across 50 real-world locations, captured with Project Aria glasses.
    最大规模户外动作数据集：264 名参与者在 50 个真实地点完成日常活动，共 300 小时，使用 Project Aria 眼镜采集。
  • Synchronized multimodal egocentric streams: First dataset pairing full-body MoCap ground truth with multiple egocentric sensors — RGB, eye-tracking, IMUs, and spatial audio — from the same device.
    同步多模态自我中心数据流：首个将全身动捕真值与同一设备上的 RGB、眼动追踪、IMU 和空间音频完整配对的数据集。
  • Largest motion-language dataset: 301.5K sentences / 8.64M words of hierarchical motion descriptions from fine-grained narrations to high-level activity summaries.
    最大动作-语言数据集：301,500 条句子 / 864 万词，覆盖从细粒度叙述到高层活动摘要的分层动作描述。
  • Comprehensive benchmarks: Evaluated SOTA algorithms on egocentric body tracking, motion synthesis, and action recognition, establishing new baselines.
    全面基准测试：在自我中心体态追踪、动作合成和动作识别上评估了当前最优算法，建立新基线。

Data Collection Pipeline

1. Capture: Participants wear Project Aria glasses while performing daily activities in the wild. A wearable MoCap suit records synchronized full-body pose at 120 fps.

2. Localization: Multi-device SLAM produces scene-scale 6-DoF trajectories for both the egocentric device and body skeleton, enabling allocentric registration.

3. Annotation: Trained annotators provide three-level language labels — frame-level narrations, paragraph descriptions, and global activity summaries — using a custom annotation tool.

4. Quality: Automated filtering removes frames with tracking loss; manual QA ensures MoCap accuracy and language coherence across all recordings.

1. 采集：参与者穿戴 Project Aria 眼镜在户外自然行动，同时穿着全身动捕服，以 120 fps 同步记录姿态。

2. 定位：多设备 SLAM 为自我中心设备和全身骨骼提供场景尺度的 6-DoF 轨迹，支持异中心（allocentric）配准。

3. 标注：专业标注员使用自定义工具，提供三层语言标签——帧级叙述、段落描述和全局活动摘要。

4. 质量：自动过滤跟踪丢失帧；人工 QA 确保所有录像中动捕精度和语言一致性。

Benchmark Tasks & Key Results

  • Egocentric body tracking: Tracks full-body pose from head-mounted IMU + RGB; Nymeria's scale significantly improves generalization over smaller lab datasets.
    自我中心体态追踪：从头戴 IMU + RGB 追踪全身姿态；Nymeria 的规模相比小型实验室数据集显著提升了泛化能力。
  • Motion synthesis: Generates future body motion conditioned on past motion and egocentric observations; large-scale training reduces distribution mismatch in in-the-wild settings.
    动作合成：以过去动作和自我中心观测为条件预测未来姿态；大规模训练减少了户外分布偏移。
  • Action recognition: Hierarchical label structure enables both fine-grained (narration) and coarse (summary) recognition; multi-modal fusion (RGB + IMU) yields best accuracy.
    动作识别：层次标签结构支持细粒度（叙述）和粗粒度（摘要）识别；RGB + IMU 多模态融合效果最佳。

TL;DR

Nymeria is the largest and most diverse egocentric motion dataset to date, covering 300 h of in-the-wild daily activities with synchronized MoCap, multimodal sensor streams, and 300K+ language annotations from 264 participants at 50 locations. It sets new baselines for body tracking, motion synthesis, and action recognition in unconstrained environments.
