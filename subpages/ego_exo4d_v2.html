Ego-Exo4D: Understanding Skilled Human Activity from First- and Third-Person Perspectives (v2 / IJCV)

Kristen Grauman, Andrew Westbury, Lorenzo Torresani, Kris Kitani, Jitendra Malik, + 95 collaborators
UT Austin · Meta FAIR · Northeastern · CMU · UC Berkeley · and 18 more institutions
Public Dataset Egocentric Ego + Exo Skilled Activities 3D Pose IJCV 2025
One-sentence problem: How can we build a large-scale multimodal benchmark that enables AI to understand skilled human activity jointly from first-person (egocentric) and third-person (exocentric) perspectives, with rich language and 3D pose supervision?
一句话问题：如何构建大规模多模态基准，使 AI 能够同时从第一人称（自我中心）和第三人称（外部视角）理解技巧性人类活动，并配备丰富的语言和 3D 姿态监督？

Key Contributions

  • Largest synchronized ego+exo dataset: 1,286 hours of video from 740 participants across 123 scenes in 13 cities worldwide, covering 8 domains (cooking, health, bike repair, music, basketball, soccer, dance, rock climbing) with 43 activities and 689 unique keysteps.
    最大规模同步 ego+exo 数据集：740 名参与者在全球 13 座城市 123 个场景中完成 8 个领域（烹饪、健康、单车维修、音乐、篮球、足球、舞蹈、攀岩）的技巧性活动，共 1,286 小时视频，43 项活动、689 个独特关键步骤。
  • Rich multimodal streams via Project Aria: RGB (1408×1408 @ 30fps), dual mono SLAM cameras, eye-tracking, 7-channel spatial audio, dual IMUs (800/1000 Hz), barometer, magnetometer — all time-synchronized and metric SLAM-localized.
    Project Aria 丰富多模态数据流：RGB（1408×1408@30fps）、双单目 SLAM 相机、眼动追踪、7 声道空间音频、双 IMU（800/1000 Hz）、气压计和磁力计，全部时间同步并经 metric SLAM 定位。
  • Three paired language corpora: (1) Expert commentary — 117,812 time-stamped segments from 52 domain experts critiquing skill execution; (2) Narrate-and-act — first-person how-to narrations by participants; (3) Atomic action descriptions — 432K sentences from third-party annotators covering every visible action.
    三种配对语言语料：（1）专家评论——52 位领域专家提供 117,812 条时间戳技能评论；（2）叙述与行动——参与者第一人称操作叙述；（3）原子动作描述——第三方标注员提供 432K 条涵盖所有可见动作的句子。
  • Four benchmark families: Ego-exo relation (correspondence + translation), Keystep recognition, Proficiency estimation, and Ego pose (body + hand), with 9.2M body and 4.3M hand 3D pose annotations (automatic) plus manually verified subsets.
    四类基准任务：自我-外部关系（对应 + 转换）、关键步骤识别、技能水平估计、自我中心姿态（全身 + 手部），含 920 万体态和 430 万手部 3D 姿态标注（自动），附手工核验子集。

Ego-exo correspondence: Match objects between ego and exo viewpoints using segmentation masks (1.8M masks, 5,566 objects). Best spatio-temporal baseline XView-XMem achieves 34.9 IoU (Ego query) and 27.1 IoU (Exo query).

Ego-exo translation: Given exo video + object masks, generate the egocentric clip. DiT-pix diffusion baseline outperforms GAN baselines on all image quality metrics (SSIM, PSNR, DISTS, LPIPS, CLIP-sim).

Proficiency estimation: Predict skill level (1–10) from video. Separate "demonstrator" scores (2,987 takes) and expert commentary with "good"/"tips" segment labels (912 takes).

Ego pose (body + hand): Recover 3D skeleton from ego video. Provides both automatic pseudo-GT (SLAM-assisted) and manually verified annotations for evaluation.
自我-外部对应：使用分割掩码（180 万掩码，5,566 个物体）匹配 ego 和 exo 视角间的物体。最优时空基线 XView-XMem 在 ego 查询时达到 34.9 IoU，exo 查询时达 27.1 IoU。

自我-外部转换：给定 exo 视频+物体掩码，生成自我中心视角片段。扩散模型基线 DiT-pix 在所有图像质量指标（SSIM、PSNR、DISTS、LPIPS、CLIP 相似度）上优于 GAN 基线。

技能水平估计：从视频预测技能水平（1–10 分），含"表演者"评分（2,987 个片段）和专家评论中的"优秀"/"提示"标签（912 个片段）。

自我中心姿态（全身+手部）：从自我中心视频恢复 3D 骨骼。提供 SLAM 辅助的自动伪真值和手工核验标注两种模式。

⚠️ Note

Ego-Exo4D provides 3D joints, not SMPL-X. So it can support 3D pose supervision/evaluation, but an extra SMPL-X fitting step is needed.

TL;DR

The IJCV 2025 journal version of Ego-Exo4D expands the CVPR 2024 paper with a larger v2 dataset, a new procedure understanding benchmark, full baseline results across all 5 task families, and comprehensive documentation of strengths/weaknesses. The core dataset (1,286h, 740 participants, 13 cities) is unchanged, but pose annotations are now fully documented with separate automatic and manual subsets — 9.2M body + 4.3M hand 3D poses (auto). Use v2 for all future work; v1 is deprecated.
