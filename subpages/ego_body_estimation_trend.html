After EgoAllo: Estimating Full-Body from Ego View

Anchor: EgoAllo (ECCV 2024) — Brent Yi et al., UC Berkeley
Egocentric Body Estimation SMPL / Whole-Body Diffusion Online / Causal CVPR 2026
EgoAllo (ECCV 2024) established conditional diffusion from egocentric SLAM as the baseline for allocentric full-body + hand estimation. Since then, at least 5 follow-up works have appeared targeting CVPR 2026 and NeurIPS 2025, pushing toward online/causal inference, camera-agnostic setups, and large-scale semi-supervised training.
EgoAllo（ECCV 2024）将自我中心 SLAM 驱动的条件扩散确立为异中心全身+手部估计的基线。此后，至少 5 篇后续工作出现，针对 CVPR 2026 和 NeurIPS 2025，推动在线/因果推理、设备无关配置和大规模半监督训练。

📌 Anchor — EgoAllo (ECCV 2024)

Estimating Body and Hand Motion in an Ego-sensed World
Brent Yi, Vickie Ye, Maya Zheng, Yunqi Li, Lea Müller, Georgios Pavlakos, Yi Ma, Jitendra Malik, Angjoo Kanazawa · UC Berkeley / ETH

Key idea: condition a diffusion model on SLAM 6-DoF head trajectory + RGB images with spatially/temporally invariant head-motion parameterization → allocentric SMPL body + MANO hands.
Results: ↓18% body MPJPE over naive conditioning; ↓40% hand error via kinematic wrist anchoring.
在自我感知世界中估计身体和手部运动
Brent Yi 等 · UC Berkeley / ETH

核心思路：以空间/时间不变的头部运动参数化条件化扩散模型，输入 SLAM 6-DoF 头部轨迹 + RGB 图像，输出异中心 SMPL 全身 + MANO 手部。
结果：相比朴素条件化，体态 MPJPE ↓18%；运动学腕部锚点使手部误差 ↓40%。

CVPR 2026 Works

CVPR 2026
EgoPoseFormer v2: Accurate Egocentric Human Motion Estimation for AR/VR
Zhenyu Li et al. · arXiv 2603.04090
Transformer with identity-conditioned queries + multi-view spatial refinement + causal temporal attention. Auto-labeling via teacher-student + uncertainty distillation on 70M unlabeled frames (EGO-ITW-70M). Benchmarked on new EgoBody3M (3.4M real frames, 4 sync cameras). Overall MPJPE 4.02 cm; outperforms EgoPoseFormer by 15.4% MPJPE and 51.7% temporal jitter. Auto-labeling adds further 13.1% wrist improvement. GPU latency: 0.8ms.
带身份条件化查询的 Transformer + 多视角空间精化 + 因果时序注意力。通过师生 + 不确定性蒸馏对 7000 万帧未标注数据（EGO-ITW-70M）进行自动标注。在新基准 EgoBody3M（340 万真实帧，4 同步相机）上测试。整体 MPJPE 4.02 cm；比 EgoPoseFormer 提升 15.4% MPJPE、51.7% 时序抖动。自动标注额外提升腕部 13.1%。GPU 延迟：0.8ms。

CVPR Findings 2026
EggHand: A Multimodal Foundation Model for Egocentric Hand Pose Forecasting
Jaeyoung Choi et al. · arXiv 2605.07642
VLA action decoder + ego video-text encoder for future hand pose prediction without requiring body pose or external tracking. SOTA on Ego-Exo4D hand forecast; robust under severe ego-motion. Language-conditioned for task-driven control.
VLA 动作解码器 + 自我中心视频-文本编码器，用于未来手部姿态预测，无需体态或外部追踪。在 Ego-Exo4D 手部预测上达到最优；在严重自我运动下保持鲁棒。支持语言条件化任务驱动控制。

Other Recent Works (2025)

arXiv 2026
EgoForce: Robust Online Egocentric Motion Reconstruction via Diffusion Forcing
Inwoo Hwang, Donggeun Lim, Hojun Jang, Young Min Kim · arXiv 2605.13041
Diffusion Forcing with per-frame asymmetric noise schedule + causal observation injection (past fully observed, current partially, future unobserved). Enables online streaming inference without fixed windows. On EE4D-Motion: MPJPE 0.073m vs EgoAllo 0.168m (↓56%) and vs HMD2 0.127m (↓43%). FID 0.016 vs EgoAllo 0.045.
扩散 Forcing 采用逐帧非对称噪声调度 + 因果观测注入（过去帧完全可观测，当前部分可观测，未来不可观测）。支持无需固定窗口的在线流式推理。在 EE4D-Motion 上：MPJPE 0.073m vs EgoAllo 0.168m（↓56%），vs HMD2 0.127m（↓43%）。FID 0.016 vs EgoAllo 0.045。

arXiv 2025
OmniEgoCap: Camera-Agnostic Sequence-Level Egocentric Motion Reconstruction
Kyungwon Cho, Hanbyul Joo · arXiv 2512.19283
Unified diffusion across diverse camera setups (fisheye, pinhole, multiple FOVs). Sequence-level inference (vs windowed) jointly recovers temporally coherent motion + consistent body shape. Geometry-aware visibility augmentation treats hand appearances as geometric constraints. Claims new SOTA on public benchmarks across hardware types.
统一扩散跨多种相机配置（鱼眼、针孔、多视场角）。序列级推理（vs 窗口化）同时恢复时序一致运动和一致体型。几何感知的可见性增强将手部外观视为几何约束。在公开基准的多种硬件类型上声称达到新 SOTA。
📄 arXiv
NeurIPS 2024
Estimating Ego-Body Pose from Doubly Sparse Egocentric Video Data
Seunggeun Chi et al. · arXiv 2411.03561
Two-stage: (1) masked autoencoder imputes sparse hand trajectories from head pose + intermittent hand observations with uncertainty; (2) conditional diffusion generates full-body motion. Evaluated on AMASS + Ego-Exo4D. Specifically targets "doubly sparse" settings where both temporal and spatial observations are incomplete.
两阶段：（1）掩码自编码器以头部姿态 + 间歇手部观测（含不确定性）补全稀疏手部轨迹；（2）条件扩散生成全身运动。在 AMASS + Ego-Exo4D 上评估。专门针对时间和空间观测均不完整的"双稀疏"场景。

TL;DR — Research Trends

Post-EgoAllo, the field is splitting into three directions:

① Online / causal inference (EgoForce, EgoPoseFormer v2) — AR/VR needs real-time streaming; Diffusion Forcing and causal attention are the emerging tools. EgoForce achieves 56% MPJPE reduction over EgoAllo while staying online.

② Camera-agnostic / large-scale (OmniEgoCap, EgoPoseFormer v2) — generalizing beyond Aria to any HMD. EgoPoseFormer v2's auto-labeling on 70M frames shows semi-supervised learning is key.

③ Sparse observation (Doubly Sparse, EgoForce) — real HMD data is often incomplete; handling intermittent hand/body observations robustly.

Common gap vs EgoAllo: none of these explicitly evaluate on HOT3D or Nymeria — EgoAllo's SMPL + world-space trajectory output remains unique in that evaluation setting.
EgoAllo 之后，该领域分化为三个方向：

① 在线/因果推理（EgoForce、EgoPoseFormer v2）——AR/VR 需要实时流式；扩散 Forcing 和因果注意力是新兴工具。EgoForce 在保持在线推理的同时，MPJPE 比 EgoAllo 降低 56%。

② 设备无关/大规模（OmniEgoCap、EgoPoseFormer v2）——从 Aria 推广到任意 HMD。EgoPoseFormer v2 在 7000 万帧上的自动标注表明半监督学习是关键。

③ 稀疏观测（Doubly Sparse、EgoForce）——真实 HMD 数据通常不完整；鲁棒处理间歇性手部/身体观测。

与 EgoAllo 共同的空白：这些工作均未在 HOT3D 或 Nymeria 上评估——EgoAllo 在世界坐标系轨迹输出的评估设定下仍具独特性。
